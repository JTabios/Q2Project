Famous Paintings of Vincent Van Gogh

Sunflowers



One of the five paintings of sunflowers Van Gogh made.

Interpreted his representation of sunflowers as gratitude.

The Starry Night



His most famous painting.

Inspired by the view in the asylum he was placed in as he was suffering from mental health issues.

A great example of his usual style of artwork.

Wheatfield
with Crows


One of his final paintings.

Thought to be a representation of the isolation he felt.

Was painted a few weeks before his death.



Irises


Series of paintings of flowers.

Were from the garden of the asylum he was in.

Influenced from ukiyo-e prints.

Self Portait


One of the many self-portait paintings of Van Gogh's career.

Showed what he would work in and very accurate features of himself.

The Bedroom in Arles


One of his most well-known paintings.

The bedroom shown is actually his bedroom itself.

The painting was for the purpose of relaxing the mind.
